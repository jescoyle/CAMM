Calculate survival probability

Usage

niche_func(x, mu, sigma)

Arguments

x
(required) vector of environmental conditions
mu
(required) vector of niche optima (matching x)
sigma
(required) vector of niche breadths (matching x)

Value

the probability of establishment

Description

Calculates the probability that a propagule will establish under specific environmental conditions

Details

The probability that a propagule will establish is the product of the probabilities that the propagule will establish under each environmental condition. (E.g. it assumes independent effects of the environmental axes.) Probabilities are calculated using a Gaussian curve whose mean is the niche optimum and whose standard deviation is the niche breadth. Curves are not standardized to be pdfs; thus, each curve reaches a maximum of 1 when the environment exactly matches a species optimum.
